Block 9 is the ninth block after the genesis block in Bitcoin's block chain. Mined by Satoshi Nakamoto on January 11, 2009, it is the oldest block to have a spent coinbase. Its bitcoins have been used in several noteworthy transactions, including the ?first transfer of bitcoins between two users.
