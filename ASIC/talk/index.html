Corrections / contributions

Feel free to make corrections and add listings, as long as you can provide some sources. A lot of the data is based on (semi-)official information, leading to things like BitFury's first chip supposedly being much more energy efficient than their second chip, based on claims. That's the sort of thing that should be near the top of things to address.

If you're not sure how the data is organized, just copy/paste from a listing that has what you need, or see ?Template:Asic for documentation of the template used. TheRealSteve (?talk) 13:33, 13 April 2015 (UTC)
