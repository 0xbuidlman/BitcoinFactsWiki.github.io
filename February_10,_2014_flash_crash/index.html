bitcoin/ February 10, 2014 flash crash
  • ?Talk

On February 10, 2014, the BTC-e bitcoin exchange rate dropped by over $500, crashing from $620 to $102 in seconds, and then rebounding within two minutes. (1.1) (1.2) (1.3) The crash is the result of what appears to be a single person selling at least 6,000 bitcoins significantly below the market price. (1.2)

The motives of the sale are a subject of debate. (1.4) As the sale was made with apparently extreme loss, (1.1) it has been suggested that it was an attempt to manipulate trading bots.
