bitcoin/ Fractional Reserve Banking and Bitcoin/ talk

Discussion of this line: "If the value of a bitcoin is stable for a long period of time, then Fractional Reserve Banking is inevitable."

Rational: If the value of a bitcoin is stable, then there will be incentive to lend bitcoins. And if there is incentive to lend, then banks will practice fractional-reserve lending to do it.
